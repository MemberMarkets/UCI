UCI Veteran Services



About

UCI Veterans Services Center (VSC) provides veterans, reservists, National Guard, active duty, and dependents assistance in obtaining the federal and state educational benefits to which they are entitled. The VSC is responsible for submitting entitlement requests for new and continuing students, answering any questions military-connected students may have concerning their educational benefits and providing resources and programs to assist these students in navigating their transition to student life.

Mission:

To provide military-connected students the world-class benefits and services they have earned – and to do so by adhering to the highest standards of compassion, commitment, excellence, professionalism, integrity, accountability, and stewardship.

Vision:

UCI Veterans Services Center is under Student Life and Leadership in the division of Student Affairs. We work collaboratively as a department to serve students from the co-curricular side of their higher education experience while ensuring that all programs have learning outcomes.

Orange County Veterans and Military Families Collaborative


Mission:

The Orange County Veterans and Military Families Collaborative (OCVMFC), a coalition of service organizations, facilitates access to comprehensive services for veterans, service members and their families.

Vision:

The vision of the OCVMFC is to continue to be recognized as the primary front door for veterans, active service members and their families for quality benefits, services and integration into the community of Orange County.

History:

Established in 2013, the OCVMFC currently consists of over 100 member service organizations that serve the needs of over 130,000 Orange County Veterans and their families. In 2018, the OCVMFC was recognized by the Department of Veterans Affairs as the Community Veteran Engagement Board for Orange County. In 2019 the University of California, Irvine, became OCVMFC's Convener, providing leadership and support to the collaborative's mission.
